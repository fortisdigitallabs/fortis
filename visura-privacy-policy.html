Privacy Policy – Visura Radio

Last updated: October 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Visura Radio mobile application (“Visura”, “App”, or “Service”) handles information.

1. Who We Are

Publisher: Visura Media (Pvt) Ltd

Developer/Technology Partner: Fortis Digital Labs (Pvt) Ltd

Contact: info@visuramedia.lk (or your preferred email)

2. What the App Does

Visura is an online radio and video streaming app. Listeners can use the app to:

  • Listen to the Visura online radio stream;
  • Browse and watch publicly available Visura YouTube channel videos (opened via web view or the YouTube app).

3. No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, store, use, or share personal information. Specifically:

  • No user registration or login;
  • No analytics or crash reporting SDKs (e.g., no Firebase Analytics/Crashlytics);
  • No advertising SDKs and no personalized advertising;
  • No contact lists, photos, precise location, or other device data;
  • No profiling, tracking, or personalization.

If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and, where required, ask for consent.

4. App Permissions

The app uses only the minimum necessary permissions to function, such as:

  • Internet/Network access – to stream audio and open external links;
  • Foreground service / media playback – to keep audio playing while the app is in the background;
  • Wake lock / audio focus – to ensure uninterrupted playback.

These permissions do not give us access to personal data; they are used solely for streaming and media controls.

5. Third-Party Content (YouTube & External Links)

The app may open the public Visura YouTube channel or other external websites. When you use those services, your use is governed by their respective privacy policies (for example, Google’s/YouTube’s policies). We do not receive any personal information from YouTube or those third parties.

See Google/YouTube policies: Google Privacy Policy.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for a general audience. Because we do not collect personal data, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

We do not collect or store personal data; therefore, there is no personal data to retain or delete. If you have any questions, contact us at privacy@visuramedia.lk.

8. Security

We do not hold personal data on our servers. Streaming infrastructure is operated with standard security controls appropriate for delivering audio/video content.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our app or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
